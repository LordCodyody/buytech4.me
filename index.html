FAQ

Straight answers.

How do you make money?

Flat fees, paid by you. No vendor commissions, no kickbacks, no affiliate strings. If a vendor offers me a referral fee, I either decline it or credit it back to you in writing.

What if I already have an IT person or MSP?

Great — I work alongside them. I'm not trying to replace ongoing support. I'm the second opinion that reviews quotes, contracts, and stack decisions before you sign.

What if I'm "too small" for an advisor?

If you're spending $150+/month on tech across your home or business, an audit usually pays for itself. If it won't, I'll tell you on the consult.

Can you really lower my cell or internet bill?

Often, yes — by switching plans, renegotiating contracts at renewal, dropping unused lines, or moving to a better-fit carrier. I don't promise a number until I see the bills.

Do you handle the actual install/setup?

Optionally. I can deliver a written plan and hand it off, or stay on to negotiate, order, and verify the rollout. Your call.

What about privacy & my data?

Your bills, contracts, and notes stay yours. I lean toward tools you can self-host or own outright wherever it makes sense, and I won't push you onto platforms that mine your data.
